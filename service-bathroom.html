BATHROOM RENOVATION

Luxury bathrooms built to last — walk-in showers, soaking tubs, heated floors, and spa-worthy finishes.

Bathroom Renovation Done Right

Transform your bathroom from a purely functional space into a personal retreat. At Sevillas New Construction, our bathroom renovation service encompasses everything from powder room updates to complete master bathroom overhauls. We specialize in creating spa-like environments that combine luxury materials with practical design.

Our experienced team handles every aspect of bathroom renovation including waterproofing, plumbing, electrical, tile work, and finishing. We use moisture-resistant materials and proven installation techniques to ensure your new bathroom looks beautiful and performs flawlessly for decades. From heated marble floors to frameless glass enclosures, we bring your vision to life.
