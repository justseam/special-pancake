HOME ADDITIONS

Seamless additions that match your existing architecture — from extra bedrooms to full second stories.

Home Additions Done Right

When your family outgrows your home, you do not have to move. Apex Construction specializes in designing and building home additions that seamlessly integrate with your existing structure. Whether you need an extra bedroom, a sunroom, a home office, or an entire second story, we ensure the addition looks like it was always part of the original design.

Our addition projects include full architectural design, structural engineering, foundation work, framing, roofing, and all interior finishes. We handle permits, inspections, and coordination with utility companies so you can focus on the exciting parts. Our team pays meticulous attention to matching existing rooflines, siding, and interior details for a cohesive result.
